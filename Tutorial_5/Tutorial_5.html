Subscribe to Pewdiepie

PewDiePie vs T-Series is an online competition between the two YouTube channels, PewDiePie (run by Felix Kjellberg) and T-Series (run by the company of the same name) for the title of the most-subscribed channel on the video-sharing website. PewDiePie has been the most-subscribed user since December 2013, while T-Series has been the most-viewed and second most-subscribed channel since early 2018.

YouTubers such as MrBeast, Markiplier, Jacksepticeye and Logan Paul have voiced their support for PewDiePie, and many of PewDiePie's fans have made efforts to gain subscribers for his channel. On the other hand, YouTubers such as CarryMinati and Jus Reign have voiced their support for T-Series.
